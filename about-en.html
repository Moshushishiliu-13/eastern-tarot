About Mystic Tarot

Explore the wisdom and magic of tarot cards

Our Story

Mystic Tarot was founded in 2024 to provide a simple and intuitive tarot reading platform for those seeking guidance. We believe that tarot is not just a tool for divination, but a mirror reflecting the inner world, inspiring self-awareness and growth.

Our team consists of professionals passionate about mysticism and psychology, dedicated to combining ancient tarot wisdom with modern psychology to offer insightful and inspiring readings.

The History of Tarot

Tarot cards originated in 15th-century Europe as a card game, later evolving into a tool for divination and self-exploration. Traditional tarot consists of 78 cards: 22 Major Arcana and 56 Minor Arcana.

The Major Arcana represents major life themes and spiritual journeys, while the Minor Arcana focuses on daily details and challenges. Each card has unique symbolism, and spreads reveal a wealth of interpretive possibilities.

Our Philosophy

At Mystic Tarot, we believe:

  • Tarot is a tool for self-reflection and exploration, not a magical prophecy of fate.
  • Everyone has free will; tarot offers possibilities and insights, but the final decision is yours.
  • Through tarot, we can connect with the wisdom of the subconscious and gain new perspectives.
  • Tarot readings should be positive, constructive, and empowering, helping people make wiser choices.
